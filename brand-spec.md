# Brand Spec: Arts Tutor (BFF Vibe)

- `--bg`: `oklch(96% 0.015 85)` (Warm Beige Paper)
- `--surface`: `oklch(100% 0 0)` (Pure White)
- `--fg`: `oklch(25% 0.02 85)` (Warm Dark Brown)
- `--accent`: `oklch(78% 0.14 55)` (Soft Orange)
- `--muted`: `oklch(60% 0.015 85)` (Stone Gray)
- `--border`: `oklch(90% 0.02 85)` (Cream Border)

## Secondary Pastels
- `--soft-blue`: `oklch(92% 0.04 240)`
- `--soft-green`: `oklch(92% 0.04 150)`
- `--soft-purple`: `oklch(92% 0.04 300)`

- **Display**: Söhne, Avenir Next, system-ui
- **Body**: SF Pro Text, system-ui

## Layout Posture
- **Radii**: 24px (very rounded, friendly)
- **Shadows**: Whisper soft `rgba(0,0,0,0.03) 0px 4px 20px`
- **Illustrations**: Hand-drawn, organic shapes, playful doodles
- **Tone**: Hinglish, friendly, calm, motivational
